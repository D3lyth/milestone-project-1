Important Information for Hogwarts Students

Below, students and parents can find some important information regarding Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Below is a wealth of information regarding the curriculum. This page includes the booklists - core and year specific books required throuout your time here at Hogwarts. All books are available to buy at Florish and Blotts on Diagon Alley, London.

Student are required to purchase the correct books for their studies. The core textbooks needed throughout your time here at Hogwarts are as follows:

  1. The Standard Book of Spells (one for each grade) by Miranda Goshawk
  2. A History of Magic by Bathilda Bagshot
  3. Magical Theory by Adalbert Waffling

As well as the core textbooks, below gives an outline of year-specific textbooks that are required:


First Year:

  1. A Beginner's Guide to Transfiguration by Emeric Switch
  2. One Thousand Magical Herbs and Fungi by Phyllida Spore
  3. Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them by Newt Scamander

Second Year:

  1. Intermediate Transfiguration by Emeric Switch
  2. Magical Drafts and Potions by Arsenius Jigger
  3. The Dark Forces: A Guide to Self-Protection by Quentin Trimble

Third Year:

  1. Transfiguration by Emeric Switch
  2. The Monster Book of Monsters by Edwardus Lima
  3. Unfogging the Future by Cassandra Vablatsky

Fourth Year (Start of OWLs):

  1. The Dark Arts: A Guide to Advanced Self-Protection by Quentin Trimble
  2. Intermediate Charms by Filius Flitwick
  3. Hogwarts: A History by Bathilda Bagshot

Fifth Year (OWLs Exam Year):

  1. Defensive Magical Theory by Wilbert Slinkhard
  2. Advanced Rune Translation by Bathsheda Babbling
  3. Confronting the Faceless by Emmeric Switch

Sixth Year (Start of NEWTs):

  1. Advanced Arithmancy by Bathilda Bagshot
  2. Advanced Potion-Making by Libatius Borage

Seventh Year (NEWTs Exam Year):

  1. The Essential Defence Against the Dark Arts by Arsenius Jigger
  2. Advanced Studies in Charms by Miranda Goshawk

We hope this list helps you prepare for the upcoming academic year. Please note that this is a required textbook list, and students may also benefit from additional reading material or supplemental texts depending on their individual interests and academic pursuits.
